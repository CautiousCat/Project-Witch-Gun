[Instructions]
- Collect the floating shotgun shells to replenish 1 shotgun ammo immediately
- While at 0 ammo on any type, touch the ground to reload that ammo type.
- While not at full ammo capacity on one or both guns, press R to reload one or both guns while on the ground.
- You can grab ledges by facing towards it while next to it.
- You can reload while on the ledge.
- Presssing E while infront of the sign board will display it's next line.

[Controls]
Enter - In-game Menu
E - Interact

A - move Left
D - move Right
R - Reload
Space Bar - Jump
Shift - Dash
Left Click - Fire Pistol
Right Click - Fire Shotgun
Hold Alt - Snap Aim to 8 Directions
